Zoe Griffiths is an artist based in Birmingham, England. She honed her skills in Painting, Drawing, Digital Art and Printmaking at Plymouth College of Art. Her artistic journey, rich in diverse influences and a keen eye for detail, reflects a mesmerizing fusion of techniques and emotions.

‘The artist’s that have inspired me throughout my journey have been Jean Michel Basquiat, Ken Kiff, Ernst Ludwig Kirchner and George Condo. These artist’s use of colour theory and imagination in their work is something that inspires me deeply. Basquiat is someone I was drawn to from the beginning of my practice, something about the way he tells his story and his experience through paint is something that really resonates with me. - Zoe
