‘In every hero's journey we embark on a quest, where we slay our own inner demons, thus changing our world forever’ (Joseph Campbell, A hero with a thousand faces) Art is my quest, where I allow myself to let go of who I think I am and embrace the movement of the paint. What comes out is always faces and always aspects of me, this intuitive process allows me to balance my opposite forces by using bold and dark colours.
